About the Managing Director

Two decades of building things that last.

Anshu founded Calliber Industries from a single machining unit into a diversified manufacturing group operating across precision engineering, industrial materials, and clean energy infrastructure. The philosophy has never changed: build for permanence, not for the next quarter.

Under his leadership, Calliber has grown into a group trusted by global industrial partners for reliability under pressure — on the factory floor and in the boardroom alike. His approach blends deep operational discipline with a long-term investment mindset, favouring compounding capability over shortcuts.

Leadership Journey

From a single unit to a global group.

2003

The first machining unit

Anshu establishes a small precision machining workshop with a five-member team, taking on subcontract work for regional automotive suppliers.

2009

Calliber Industries incorporated

The workshop is formalised into Calliber Industries, expanding into industrial component manufacturing with its first dedicated production facility.

2014

Entry into industrial materials

Calliber diversifies into industrial materials processing, securing its first export contracts across Southeast Asia and the Middle East.

2019

Clean energy infrastructure division launched

Recognising the shift in industrial demand, Anshu leads Calliber into renewable-linked infrastructure manufacturing, a division that now anchors group growth.

2024

Global expansion milestone

Calliber crosses operations in 14 countries, with three flagship manufacturing campuses and a group-wide sustainability charter.
